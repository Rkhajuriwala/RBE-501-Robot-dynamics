Question 1 Torque equation

syms m q l dq g Dq t th1(t) th2(t)

End effector position

here x(1) is actually l*cos(pi/2-q) some i have taken it as l*sinq

x1 =[l*sin(q);
     l*sin(q);];

Velocity

v = jacobian(x1,[q])* [dq];

Kinetic Energy

k = 0.5 * m * (v.' * v);

Potential Energy

p = - m*g*l*cos(q);

lagrangian (L)

L = simplify(k-p);

euler- langrange equation

tau = d/dt(dL/d(dq)) -dL/dq

For the first part of the equation i.e d/dt(dL/d(dq))

dL_dq = diff(L,dq);
dL_dq_sub= subs(dL_dq,[q dq],[th1 diff(th1(t),t)]);
dL1 = diff(dL_dq_sub,t);
dL1_a = subs(dL1,[th1 diff(th1(t),t) diff(th1(t),t,t)],[q dq Dq]);

for the second part of the equation i.e dL/dq

d_L_q = diff(L,q);

Tau

Tau = simplify(d_L_dq - d_L_q)

 
Tau =
 
l*m*(g*sin(q) + 2*dq*l*cos(q)^2 + 2*dq^2*l*cos(q)*sin(q))

Inertia matrix M

M = simplify(Tau - subs(Tau,Dq,0))/Dq

 
M =
 
0

Gravity G

G = subs(Tau, [dq Dq],[0 0])

 
G =
 
g*l*m*sin(q)

Coriolis Coupling Term

C = Tau- (M* [Dq].' + G)

 
C =
 
l*m*(g*sin(q) + 2*dq*l*cos(q)^2 + 2*dq^2*l*cos(q)*sin(q)) - g*l*m*sin(q)
